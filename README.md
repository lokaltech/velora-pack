# Velora Pack

Modern corporate website for a plastic packaging manufacturer specializing in OPP packaging solutions.

Velora Pack is a fictional B2B manufacturing company created as a portfolio project for LokalTech. The website is designed to demonstrate how traditional manufacturing businesses can improve their digital presence, showcase production capabilities, and generate qualified leads through a professional website experience.

---

## Overview

Many manufacturing companies still rely on outdated websites or social media platforms as their primary online presence.

Velora Pack demonstrates a modern alternative:

- Professional corporate branding
- Product showcase
- Manufacturing capabilities
- Production capacity information
- Certifications and compliance
- Lead generation through inquiry forms and WhatsApp integration
- SEO-ready architecture

The goal is to help manufacturers build trust with potential buyers, distributors, and business partners.

---

## Features

### Landing Page

Modern industrial-themed landing page designed to communicate trust, scale, and professionalism.

### Product Catalog

Showcase packaging products including:

- OPP Plastic Bags
- Custom Printed Packaging
- Garment Packaging
- Food Packaging
- Flexible Packaging Solutions

### Manufacturing Capabilities

Display production processes and machinery capabilities such as:

- Extrusion
- Printing
- Lamination
- Slitting
- Bag Making
- Quality Control

### Production Capacity

Business-critical information including:

- Monthly production capacity
- Number of production lines
- Operational hours
- Custom MOQ support

### Industries Served

Target industries include:

- Food & Beverage
- Fashion & Garment
- Cosmetics
- Retail
- E-Commerce
- Industrial Supplies

### Certifications

Dedicated section for displaying:

- ISO Certifications
- Food Grade Compliance
- Environmental Standards
- Industry Certifications

### Portfolio Showcase

Gallery showcasing completed packaging projects and product applications.

### Inquiry Form

Potential customers can submit inquiries directly through the website.

### WhatsApp Integration

Form submissions can be converted into a structured WhatsApp message, allowing direct communication with the sales team.

Example:

Name: PT Maju Bersama
Product: OPP Packaging
Quantity: 50,000 pcs

Requesting quotation and lead time information.

### SEO-Ready Blog

Educational articles designed to improve organic search visibility.

Examples:

- OPP vs PE Plastic Packaging
- Packaging Trends 2026
- Benefits of Custom Printed Packaging
- How to Choose Food Grade Packaging
